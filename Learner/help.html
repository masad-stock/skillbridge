Getting Started

To create an account, click the "Sign Up" button on the homepage. Fill in your email address, create a password, and provide basic information about yourself. You'll receive a confirmation email to verify your account.

Use the navigation menu at the top to access different sections. Your dashboard shows your enrolled courses, progress, and recommendations. The search bar helps you find specific courses or topics.

Account & Billing

Go to your account settings and select "Payment Methods." You can add, update, or remove payment cards and view your billing history from this section.

Learning Experience

Your progress is automatically tracked as you complete lessons and quizzes. You can view your progress in the course dashboard, which shows completed modules and overall completion percentage.

Technical Support

Our platform works on most modern devices. For optimal performance, we recommend:
  • Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
  • Stable internet connection (minimum 2 Mbps)
  • Device with at least 4GB RAM
